- [Modifiche di rilievo in Roslyn 2,0 (VS2017) da Roslyn 1. * (VS2015) e C# dal compilatore nativo (VS2013 e precedenti).](https://github.com/dotnet/roslyn/blob/master/docs/compilers/CSharp/Compiler%20Breaking%20Changes%20-%20VS2017.md)
- [Modifiche di rilievo in Roslyn 1,0 (VS2015) dal compilatore C# nativo (VS2013 e precedenti).](https://github.com/dotnet/roslyn/blob/master/docs/compilers/CSharp/Compiler%20Breaking%20Changes%20-%20VS2015.md)
